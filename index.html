Our Story

Founded on a passion for authentic French baking, Artisan Boulangerie brings centuries-old techniques to Berkeley Heights. Our master baker trained in Lyon, France, where he learned the sacred art of bread making from fourth-generation bakers.

Every morning before dawn, our artisans begin the careful process of shaping, proofing, and baking. We use only the finest ingredients: European butter, organic stone-ground flours, and wild yeast starters that have been lovingly maintained for over a decade.

From our signature heritage sourdough to delicate French pastries, each creation reflects our commitment to excellence and our respect for tradition. We believe that great baking is both an art and a science—requiring patience, skill, and an unwavering dedication to quality.
