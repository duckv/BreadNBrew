Our Story

Founded with a passion for exceptional coffee and artisan baking, Bread N' Brew has become Berkeley Heights' beloved destination for quality and community.

Our skilled bakers start early each morning, hand-crafting fresh breads, pastries, and treats using time-honored techniques and premium ingredients. Every cup of coffee is expertly brewed from carefully selected beans.

Whether you're grabbing your morning coffee, enjoying a leisurely breakfast, or picking up fresh bread for dinner, we're here to make every visit special.
